# Score Analysis

## Distribution

Most wallets score between 400–800. A few are risky (0–100), and some are highly trustworthy (900+).

## High-Score Behavior

- Regular deposits and repayments
- Long-term activity
- No liquidations

## Low-Score Behavior

- Got liquidated often
- Did not repay loans
- Very short or no activity
